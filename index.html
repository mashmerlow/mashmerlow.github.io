updates.

Website rework! Finally!! — 8th of April 2025
After years of messing around with designing and imagining a new layout for this website, I think I'm happy with what I've got.

While before I had been more or less trying to recreate a "carrd.co"-looking UI, I always felt really constrained by it, and especially by the bad practices and hacks I had to do to make it work how I wanted. Now it's no longer something I have to worry about. It's wholly remade from the ground up, in a more flexible form, while still having a distinctive style.

Speaking of which, the style I'm aiming for is kind of a mix of older Internet with a "newer" look.

Hope you like it!!
